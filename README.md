# SkillKingdom

## 🧭 Overview

**SkillKingdom** is a class-based progression game where players develop both **combat mastery** and **crafting expertise** through a dual-path system.

Players begin their journey by choosing:

* A **Class** (combat identity)
* A **Profession** (crafting/economic identity)

Both paths level independently, allowing for flexible builds and long-term progression.

---

## 🎮 Character Creation

At the start of the game, players must:

### 1. Enter Character Name

* Free-form input
* Used for saves and in-game identity

---

### 2. Choose a Class

Classes define combat style, stat growth, and abilities.

#### ⚔️ Warrior

* Focus: Strength, Vitality, Endurance
* Playstyle: Durable melee combatant
* Strengths: High survivability, consistent damage
* Weaknesses: Limited range, low utility

#### 🔮 Mage

* Focus: Intelligence, Wisdom, Control
* Playstyle: High-damage spellcaster
* Strengths: Burst damage, elemental versatility
* Weaknesses: Fragile, resource-dependent

#### 🏹 Hunter

* Focus: Agility, Perception, Reaction
* Playstyle: Ranged precision and mobility
* Strengths: High accuracy, evasion
* Weaknesses: Lower durability, positioning reliant

---

### 3. Choose a Profession

Professions define how players gather resources and craft items.

All professions begin at **Level 0** and level independently from class.

---

## 🛠️ Professions

### Core Professions

#### 🔨 Smith

* Crafts weapons and armor
* Uses ore and metal materials

#### 🪵 Woodworker

* Crafts bows, staves, and tools
* Uses wood and natural materials

#### ✨ Enchanter

* Enhances gear with magical properties
* Applies permanent upgrades

#### 🧪 Alchemist

* Creates potions and consumables
* Focus on healing and buffs

#### 🪶 Runist

* Inscribes runes onto equipment
* Creates modular enhancements

---

### Expanded Professions (Planned)

* 🔧 Tinkerer — gadgets, traps, mechanical items
* ⛏️ Miner — gathers ore and rare materials
* 🌿 Herbalist — gathers plants for alchemy
* 🐾 Trapper — collects hides and creature materials
* 📜 Scribe — creates scrolls and magical texts
* 🍖 Cook — creates food-based buffs

---

## ⚖️ Core Design Philosophy

* **Class = Combat Power**
* **Profession = Crafting & Economy**

This separation allows:

* Clear progression paths
* Build diversity
* Scalable systems for future expansion

---

## 📈 Progression

Players start with:

* Class Level: **0**
* Profession Level: **0**

Progression unlocks:

* New abilities (Class)
* New recipes/tools (Profession)
* Increased efficiency and output
